Who We Are

I don't care enough about you to lie to you. This could be seen as an odd approach for a relationship counselor to take when first meeting you, but at Therapy Trails we believe in honesty.

We aren't choosing sides. We aren't forcing anyone to "stick it out". We start with observation. How stress, joy, being lost or found on a quest in the city or a journey in the woods, presents itself. We go from there.

Who You Are

"Two roads diverged in a wood, and I --I took the one less traveled by, and that has made all the difference."

These well-known words by poet, Robert Frost, hold truth for you and millions of other couples worldwide. Having conflict in your relationship is not unique, but actively choosing a path to healing and wholeness is...

Welcome to the Path less traveled! Let's make all the difference.
